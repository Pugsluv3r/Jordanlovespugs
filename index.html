About Me

Hi, I'm Pugs! (or Jordan) i'm a passionate web developer, modder, reverse engineer, and game developer.

My Journey

I started my journey in web development in a math class in grade 8. On the school chromebooks, text app (Yes the one with no autocomplete) this is where i learnt the basics of html, css, and javascript. Eventually I moved to a VScode (the web browser version). Around this time I also got into modding the game, gorilla tag. While modding, I learnt the basic fundamentals of C# and gathered alot of close friends. This eventually led to my interest in game development, I learnt the engine Unity as, it is a good entry level engine. Eventually, I also became interested in reverse engineering, which allowed me to create more advanced mods and understand the inner workings of the games I played. After that I gained co-director status in a project, this gave me to manage communitys.
